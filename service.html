Our Services

Assisted Living at Home

  • This Program helps you remain independently at home for as long as possible. This is perfect for the busy elderly who, while enjoying their golden years but need some help with the things they are too busy to do, do not want to do themselves or are unable to do. This program allows you to age happily and gracefully.

Post Hospital & Rehab Care

  • Returning home from the hospital or a Rehab can be daunting. This service allows you to safely transition from a facility to home. This service is perfect for someone who is currently hospitalized or is in Rehab but planning to go home safely.

Long Distance Care Giving

  • This service is perfect for that person whose closest relatives or family member who is the primary Caregiver lives an hour or more away from them. The service takes the stress out of long-distance caregiving.

Dementia & Alzheimer’s Program

  • Our Dementia & Alzheimer’s Care Program helps to create a nurturing environment for the individual affected by Dementia & Alzheimer.

Respite Care

  • Our Respite care Program gives families’ caregivers break without feeling guilty and knowing that their family member is ok.

Companionship

  • Unmet social needs can negatively impact health. This service helps to meet the social needs of the elderly by interacting with them, planning social outings and trips, playing games and cards, discussing current and historical events.
