About Us

WE ARE NURSE-OWNED

WE HAVE NO MINIMUM HOUR

Matilda Oyedele founded Caring Heart and Hand. She says: “I have worked as a bedside nurse for eight years and realized that lots of the elderly keep coming to hospital because they do not have anyone to care for them at home. Knowing that most elderly want to remain in the comfort of their home where they have created lots of memories, where their kids, grandchildren, relatives, and friends come and go for holidays, parties, and vacations. I began the quest to find a way to help the elderly in their homes without having them in hospitals or a nursing home unless it is necessary, this was what gave birth to Caring Heart and Hand. Our services help you age gracefully and happily in the comfort of your home while keeping your independence”

Matilda has over eight years of experience as a nurse and in the Homecare Industry. She graduated with her Bachelor Science in Nursing at Western Governor University, Utah. Her Mission is providing personalized Caregiving for senior living.
